Popular in Terengganu


Nasi Lemak

Nasi lemak is a traditional and popular Malaysian dish that consists of fragrant rice
cooked in coconut milk and pandan leaves. The name "nasi lemak" translates to "rich rice" in English,
which reflects the creamy and aromatic nature of the dish.
















Popular in Terengganu


Nasi Dagang

Nasi dagang is a traditional Malaysian dish that originates from the east coast states of Malaysia,
particularly from the states of Terengganu and Kelantan. It is a flavorful and aromatic dish consisting
of steamed rice that is cooked in a mixture of coconut milk and various spices, resulting in a slightly creamy
and fragrant rice.
















Popular in Terengganu


Nasi Kerabu

Nasi kerabu is a flavorful and vibrant Malaysian dish that hails from the northeastern states
of Malaysia, particularly Kelantan and Terengganu. It is known for its striking blue-colored rice,
which is achieved using a natural coloring derived from the petals of the butterfly pea flower (bunga telang).
The blue-hued rice is the hallmark of nasi kerabu and gives it a unique appearance.

Ayam Gepuk

Ayam gepuk is an Indonesian dish that features fried chicken that has been marinated, pounded,
and then fried until crispy. The name "ayam gepuk" translates to "smashed chicken" in English,
referring to the pounding or flattening of the chicken before cooking.
















Popular in Terengganu


Mee Kari

Mee kari, also known as curry noodles, is a popular dish in Malaysia and other parts of Southeast Asia.
It consists of yellow egg noodles served in a flavorful curry broth that's often made with a variety of spices,
coconut milk, and sometimes a protein such as chicken, beef, shrimp, or tofu.
